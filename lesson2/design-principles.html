Visual Hierarchy

byui.edu

We can see the Visual Hierarchy principle for two main reasons. 1) The BYU logo due it is at the left-top of the page having any visitor know the institution's name. 2). The first box after the name is probably the most relevant and in tendency news; Covid's information.

Fitt's Law

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

churchofjesuschrist.org

We can see Fitt's Law applied in the button tab. If we notice, for a right-handed user who is holding a smartphone, the buttons search, languages, library, and login are the most accessible buttons because of its location, and proximity.
